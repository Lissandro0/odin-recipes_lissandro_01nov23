Hotdogs

Description:

  • The hot dog is a food in the form of a sandwich that is generated by the combination of a boiled or fried sausage, served in an elongated bun that is usually accompanied with some dressing, such as ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise, jalape√±o, onion, and sauerkraut.

    • Ingredients:

    1. Teaspoons of vegetable oil ---------- 2
    2. Meat or pork sausages -------------- 6
    3. Hot dog buns ------------------------ 6
    4. Cup onion, finely chopped ----------- 1/2
    5. Cup cabbage ------------------------ 1
    6. Ketchup, to taste
    7. Mayonnaise, to taste
    8. Mustard, to taste
    9. Mustard, to taste
    10. Corn sauce, to taste
    11. Crispy chips on sticks, to taste

    How to make hotdogs:

    • Heat the oil in a medium skillet over medium heat. Cook the sausages until cooked and lightly browned. Remove them from the pan and place them on absorbent paper.
    • Open the hot dog buns lengthwise.
    • Place the sausages inside the bread. Add the onion, cabbage, tomato sauce, mayonnaise, mustard and corn sauce, to taste. Sprinkle with crispy chips and grated yellow cheese, if desired.
    • Serve immediately.
